# Driver Application Form Setup

The driver application form uses **FormSubmit.co** to send applications directly to your company email (peke9013@gmail.com). No email client opens — applicants submit on the site and see a confirmation message.

## One-time activation

The first time someone submits an application, FormSubmit will send an **activation email** to peke9013@gmail.com. Click the activation link in that email once. After that, all future submissions will arrive automatically.
