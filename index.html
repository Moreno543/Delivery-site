About T & H Delivery

T & H Delivery is a professional logistics provider specializing in the secure transport of auction merchandise from warehouse to residence.

Official delivery partner

Through our partnership with Nellis Auction, we provide white-glove delivery service directly to your home, eliminating warehouse pickup and wait times.

Service overview

  • Professional warehouse pickup and transport
  • Fleet matched to load specifications
  • Photo documentation upon delivery
  • Coordinated loading at designated warehouse areas

Transparent pricing

  • Medium — $19.99
  • Large — $29.99
  • Extra-large — $39.99

How it works

A streamlined process from auction win to doorstep delivery.

1. Place your bid

Browse and bid on inventory at Nellis Auction.

2. Select delivery at checkout

Add weekend delivery at cost during checkout, avoiding warehouse pickup and in-person wait times.

3. Weekend delivery window

Deliveries are scheduled Friday through Sunday. Your order will arrive at your address on one of these three days.
