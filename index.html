Auction item delivery made simple.

T & H Delivery specializes in auction pickups. We collect your items from the auction warehouse, load them into the right-size vehicle, and deliver to your home or business with photo proof.

Pricing starts at $9.99 small, $19.99 medium, $29.99 large, and $39.99 extra-large for single items. For bulk loads (like 40+ smalls or mixed sizes), request a custom quote.

We offer big vehicles, prior-day pickups for next-day delivery, and emailed pictures when your items are dropped off. We do require a designated pickup area inside the auction warehouse for safe loading.

How it works

Three quick steps from auction pickup to delivery.

1) Request a quote

Send pickup and delivery info along with item details (dimensions or photos help).

2) We schedule pickup

We coordinate a pickup window with the auction company and confirm a designated loading area in their warehouse.

3) Delivery & proof

We use the right-size vehicle to deliver to your address and email photos as proof of delivery.

Get a quote

If you don’t know the dimensions/weight, send photos and any notes—we can still quote.

Helpful to include

  • Pickup auction company + address
  • Pickup hours and warehouse pickup area
  • Item size/weight (approx)
  • Stairs, elevator, or gate code
